League Homepage

Your League Homepage

netGolfLeague uses a flexible widget system to display everything your members need — upcoming events, recent results, scores, and standings.

Overview

Understanding the Widget System

NGL builds your league homepage from a system of content widgets — modular panels that each display a specific type of information. Your members see the most relevant data at a glance without needing to dig through menus.

The widgets automatically update as you post scores and schedule new events. There's no manual publishing step — everything is live the moment you save.

Customizing Your Homepage

To change which widgets appear or how they look, go to Setup → Change Season Configuration. From there you can toggle different scoring views and adjust what's displayed on the main page.

Widget

Next Scheduled Event

This widget highlights the upcoming round so your golfers always know what's coming. It includes:

  • Event date and name
  • Course directions — powered by MapQuest
  • Match pairings — who's playing who
  • Weather forecast — conditions for the day
Tip: Pairings must be created in the Schedule section before they appear in this widget. Pairings are also required for player names to show on the scorecard entry screen.
Widget

Last Completed Event

After a round is posted, the Last Completed Event widget shows your members the fresh results. It links directly to the scorecard for that round.

Use the Select Event View widget (the Schedule section) to browse and access any previous round from the season.

Scorecards

Scorecard Viewing Options

NGL offers two ways to view a round's scorecard:

Standard View

Shows each player's raw scores exactly as they were entered — no handicap adjustments. Great for seeing actual hole-by-hole performance.

Net Score View

Displays each player's net score per hole — their actual score minus their handicap strokes. This is the most common view for competitive leagues.

Team Scoring Views

For team leagues, you can display results in two formats:

  • Total Points — cumulative points across all matches
  • Wins / Losses — head-to-head match record

Switch between views in Setup → Change Season Configuration.

Navigation

Switching Seasons & Leagues

If you manage multiple seasons or belong to more than one league, switching between them is simple.

1

Open the Main Menu

Click the Main Menu link at the top of your league homepage.

2

Select Add/Change Season

Choose Add/Change Season to see all available seasons for the current league.

3

Switch League (if needed)

Members who belong to multiple leagues can also switch leagues from the same menu option.
